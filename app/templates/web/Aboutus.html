OUR STORYS


Implicity never goes out of style - this has been our philosophy at Shanti and one of the many things that brought us together! Our frustration with the lack of this simplicity in the fine jewellery we owned made us think of InfyNow. All the pieces we owned were heavy, kept in our bank lockers and taken out only for special occasions. We realized this was the case with most girls in our generation and so decided to build something that every girl has most likely looked for but couldn’t find –Fine jewellery that can be worn every day, that the modern girl feels proud to own, and that doesn’t break the bank!At InfyNow you will find jewellery in 18 kt gold, which is classic, minimalist and contemporary that you can enjoy wearing everyday, while dressing up or down!

WHY Jewellery


Luxury should be fun, affordable and part of your everyday. Crafted in 18k gold and diamonds, our pieces are luxurious yet light, hassle-free and easy to wear. Based out of Mumbai, India, InfyNo was born with the idea to create pieces that can go from the breakfast table to the dance floor, from your office wardrobe to your indianwear, from a simple look to a Shanti’d bolder look!The versatility of InfyNo is what sets it apart - we are here to complement your style, not define it!At Shanti, we truly believe that you don’t need an occasion to wear fine jewellery!!
